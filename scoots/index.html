Scoots offers great rentals for vehicles that you can use to navigate your way around the island. Scoots offers half and full day rentals that you can reserve or pay for with walk in options. We offer Honda vehicles as well as Jeep. You can get a single scooter for yourself or a two seater. We also offer ATV and Jeep Wrangler vehicles to fit up to five people for families or friends. Prices range from as low as $20 to $60 for half day rentals and $30 to $125 for full day rentals.
