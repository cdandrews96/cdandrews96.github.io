PARC: Repitition

Little Caesars

Little Caesars website

I think that little caesars has a good design principle for repetition. Repetition is used for branding your company by its style. Little Caesars uses repition in their color scheme. The orange color that they used is repeated for every window box and even in the fonts that they use.

Visual Heiarchy

Coolors

Coolors.co

Coolors shows a how visual hiearchy can be used to lead the eye to see what the creater wants you to see first. On this front page the hiearchy of the line "The super fast color scheme generator!" is where the eye is lead first to help you simply understand what this website is about. Then your eye is lead next to the button that starts the generator where it takes you to the whole purpose of the site.
