Target Audience

My target audience for Petal Weather is cyclists. They around the age of 18-40. It will be a nice designed site for cyclists to see what is going on with the weather and news about the weather with cycling.

Personas

Jake

Jake is an architect. He is about 38. One of his hobbies is cycling. He does it to work out and likes to go on big cross country trips with his friends. They do these trips every few months. They plan each trip with stuff they need and a week before they check the weather to see what the condition will be during the trip.

Sarah

Sarah is about 26 years old. She works at the Hospital as a nurse. She is starting to bike to work each morning. She uses basic apps to check the weather each morning. If it is raining she would prefer not to bike to work on those days.

Scenarios

  • A cyclist is planning his next week for work outs and times to go bike. He wants to know the best days to do those.
  • Jake is planning on a cross country bike trip with friends. He needs to know how to prepare each day of the trip.
  • Hannah is planning an all day bike trip. She will need to keep track of the weather throughout the day.
  • A college kid bikes to class. He needs to know what the weather will be like everyday.
  • Jake is planning on biking to work from now on. He will need to know what days to bike to work and what days not to.
